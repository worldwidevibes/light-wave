Music

Bad Blood by Nao

The song starts off with a positive vibe and gets even better when the beat drops. The song has a very techno yet classy feel to it. Nao used her vocals to create a contrast so that the song doesn't become repetitive.


Hot Bling Cover by Alex Aiono

The popular hit song "One Dance" originally by Drake is taken into a different direction in this cover. Aiono not only adds a beat that makes it unique but also adds in a part of the song "Hasta el Amanecer" by Nicky Jam that fit perfect with the song.

Poetry

"LOST ONES"
Gwen Leilani
As I Sit Here In This Broken Down State Of Mind,
Broken Because Of The Love Of A Lost One
I Think Of Why I Am Broken Down , What Were The Thoughts Of This Lost One ?
He Was 6’2 , With The Eyes Of A Smooth Operator
Words So Smooth , He Could Make You Change Your Mind , A Manipultive Type Of Persuader
Why Did I Let You Feed Me Thorns , But Thanked You For Roses
Why Did I Let You Lead , When Our Road Wasn't Chosen
Why Did I Always Stay & Believe In All Of Your Damn Lies
Probably Because I'm A Lost One
Who found A Cool Vibe.
"A LETTER TO LOVE"
Ni Aikoo
Don't mean to spite you
Might turn around and fight you
See love now I despise you because I found myself to be just like you
Good for the mind and bad for the soul, Love do you know how many stories have been told
About you?
Dear love I hate you
And I know you feel same
Damn love
Don't think I'll ever forgive you
When I die and come back I hope I never relive you
How dare you intrude on me
Haven't done much to you yet you're so rude to me
Ain't you supposed to be the pain reliever?
Yet causing pain is all you do to me
Dear love you're a sin
Not sure who's worse between you and him
But in hindsight you two are one of the same
Both the makers of your own twisted game
And I don't mean to say your name in vain
But I'm here now and you're the one to blame
Only one part of me that's untouched by you is my mind
So don't destroy that piece of mine
Dear love I wish I would've never met you Sincerely, the one you should've left behind
"COLORS"
Jade The Poet
People are all beautiful no matter the color
Your mother, your teacher, your father, your brother
Albino is beauty
Skin as dark as the sky on a night in the suburbs
Skin brown as melted chocolate on a s'more
Skin with different pigments
These people embody the beauty and grace of those who are not the face on the cover of the magazines
Though they are kings and queens
the people who fail to see this are the media and the people who run it
the government The source of all hell in America
The father of hate and the lighter of torture
Mixed race people?
They truly do exist but not everyone knows or understand why people must inter breed with a skin color or ethnicity different from themselves
We are Human, We are valid, We don't need to prove ourselves to be a part of your race
We aren't your fetishes
You stare at me in disgrace
While looking at my mother's brown face
I feel somewhat displaced
Maybe I'm not a part of this race?
Where do I belong in this database in the desktop of life
How can I identify myself if I'm , “ Basically white”?
To be exact I'm Puerto Rican, Jamaican, Irish, Scottish, Cuban, Italian, Taino Indian,and Black
How do I proudly represent myself when my own race is not represented in a questionnaire
Sometimes I wonder why the worlds so unfair
Does my fair skin determine the race that I bare?
Colors are so complicated you never know what they represent
If you misinterpret someone's identity you may just leave a dent.
