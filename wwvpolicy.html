Our Policy



The WorldWideVibes goal is to provide a positive environment for people of all ages. We strive our best not to cultivate hate in the WorldWideVibes community. By using our servivces, you agree to follow our rules.

Rules

Users may not show hate or discrimination towards any user, standard or administrating. Users may not bully or harass others. Users may not reveal or give personal information publicly which includes, but is not limited to: phone numbers, passwords, street or mail addresses, personal email addresses, etc. Users may not post mature content without a warning for younger audiences and audiences that may be disturbed by it.

Consequences

Failure to follow these rules or to agree to these terms of service may result in the deletion of your account or artwork. In some cases, it may involve law enforcement.
